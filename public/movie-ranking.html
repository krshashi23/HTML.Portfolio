The best Movies According to Shashi

My Top 3 movies of all time:

3-IDIOTS

One of the outstanding movie bollywood has ever created which covers the entire lifestyles of Bachelors, especially Engineers. Also showed how aspirants have to deal with their mental pressures.

TAARE ZAMEEN PAR

The movie taare zameen par, here the character of abnormal student revels, every aspirant needs a SARATHI, as teacher in life when he/she goes through their bad Phases.

12TH FAIL

The Biopic, 12TH Fail incorporates the life journey of IPS officer Manoj Kumar Sharma fearlessly embraces the idea of restarting his academic journey and reclaiming his destiny at a place where millions of students attempt the world's toughest competitive exam: UPSC.
